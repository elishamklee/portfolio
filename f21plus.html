Microsite

UI/UX Design
Forever 21 website has 4 main categories such as Women, Men, Plus Size, and Kids. Recently, e-commerce consumer's needs and shopping behavior are changing rapidly. Personalized shopping experience is the most notable change in e-commerce. Executive team wanted to build each microsites to more aggressive approach to each target audience.

Problems

Forever 21 Apps are Hybrid App. Hybrid apps are essentially web apps that have been put in a native app shell. Forever 21 website is not RWD (Responsive Web Design) so they need to build the mobile website for mobile & app devices. However, mobile website UI/UX dosen't fit in App platform and there are lots of perfomance issues.

Solution

Create new Native Forever 21 Apps to enhance UI/UX and performance for App users. Following each device standard guideline and defining new UI/UX for Forever 21 Apps. Also, collaborat with App deverpers how to improve performance.
