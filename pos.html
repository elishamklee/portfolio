POS

Cannabis Industry haven't had the standard UI/UX design before. Since Cannabis has been legalized for recreational purposes in certain states, they need to revamp website and build new App. Therefore, MedMen started to define their own UI Style Guide to create digital products for across multiple platform.

Problems

Apple Team and vendors are suffering from resizing symbols and creating consistent design. Therefore, designs were inconsistent and it's not easy to update. Also, there was any tutorial at all so new members couldn't understand design system.

Solution

I created Vuekit and Polairs library with "Smart Layout" from scratch. "Smart Layout" is a new feature of Sketch. This feature makes Symbols much more flexible, resizable, and manageable for consistent design. Moreover, I created a video tutorial for Junior and new design members.
